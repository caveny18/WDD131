My Wedding - A Platform for Contributing to Gifts

Site Name

Name: My Wedding - A Platform for Contributing to Gifts

Reason: The site provides an organized way for guests to contribute financially towards wedding gifts rather than bringing physical gifts.

Site Purpose

The website serves as a hub for wedding details and allows guests to contribute towards wedding gifts. It features gift goals with progress bars, a contribution form, and a thank-you/contact section.

Scenarios

  • How can I contribute towards a specific wedding gift?
  • What are the details and schedule of the wedding?

Color Schema

  • #b56576 - Used for headings and key accents.
  • #f8f8f8 - Background color.

Typography

  • Arial, sans-serif - Used for body text and content.

Wireframes

Mobile View: Simple layout with a navigation bar, wedding details, and a gift contribution section.

Desktop View: Wider layout with a sidebar for easy navigation and a detailed gift contribution tracker.
